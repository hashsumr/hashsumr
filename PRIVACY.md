# hashsumr Privacy Policy
Last Updated: 2026-01-28

1. Introduction
   We value your privacy. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information through the use of hashsumr.

2. Data Collection and Use
   - No Personal Information Collected: This application does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from its users.
   - No Permissions: The application does not require access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or location data.

3. Third-Party Services
   We do not share any data with third-party services. The application operates entirely locally on your device.

4. Data Security
   Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no risk of your personal data being leaked from our servers. We rely on the security features of the Windows operating system to protect any local application data.

5. Changes to This Policy
   We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact Us
   If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us by opening an issue on our [GitHub Issue Tracker](https://github.com/hashsumr/hashsumr/issues).
